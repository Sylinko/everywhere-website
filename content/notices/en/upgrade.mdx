⏱️ This update is expected to be completed between **June 8 and June 14, 2026**. Prior to this, subscription to the Pro plan will be temporarily unavailable.

## 💡 Changes

### Increase in Monthly Credits for All Plans

We will fully upgrade the monthly available credits for the Starter, Plus, and Pro plans:

| Plan | Original Monthly Credits | New Monthly Credits | Monthly Fee |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Starter** | 4M | **5M** | Remains unchanged (US$10/mo) |
| **Plus** | 10M | **12M** | Remains unchanged (US$20/mo) |
| **Pro** | 24M | **36M** | Adjusted to US$60/mo |

### Introduction of Short-Term Usage Quotas

While significantly increasing the total monthly credits, we are introducing "5-hour" and "weekly" usage quotas for **certain high-cost models**.

| Plan | 5-Hour Limit | Weekly Limit | Relative Ratio |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Starter** | ~ 226 requests | ~ 565 requests | 1× |
| **Plus** | ~ 542 requests | ~ 1,356 requests | 2.4× |
| **Pro** | ~ 1,627 requests | ~ 4,068 requests | 7.2× |

*Estimated based on average usage data for GPT-5.4 mini.*  
*(2,000 input tokens, 12,000 cached tokens, and 240 output tokens per request).*

## 🟢 Models Exempt from Quota Limits

Not all models will be subject to the aforementioned restrictions. Taking into account cost and cache hit performance, the following models **will only consume your monthly credits and will not count towards the 5-hour and weekly quotas**:

* GPT-5.4 nano
* Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite
* DeepSeek V4 Pro / Flash
* MiniMax-M2.7 / M2.5

*Once the adjustment is complete, the pricing page will update the specific list of restricted models in real time.*
